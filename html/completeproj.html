Commercial Project: Rockhampton Leagues Club

Log-in Form for Booking System. Connected to Firestore JSON real-time database

Custom SVG graphics in photoshop, tweaked an optimised alongside Vuetifyjs to provide as much data as possible. However, as I reflect on accessibility I would reconsider heavy use of color coding for data representation.

Data validation, with hints and prompts for invalid data-types. Variables are instantiated at the most common booking attributes: midday, 2 people, etc.
